AI in the Film Industry

MAD(A) Project Homepage

For my portfolio, I'll be discussing the use of Artificial Intelligence in the film industry and its various uses, drawbacks, and advantages. The topics I'll be focusing on are AI in scriptwriting, AI in green lighting scripts/commercial viability, AI in VFX/editing, and AI and actors. For my design, I'll be using a cohesive structure with consistent fonts, colours, themes, and graphics to contribute to a better user experience. I want to provide an overview on AI and its creative uses for an audience with less knowledge, curious about the subject and looking to learn more.
