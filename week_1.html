Task 2: Explore the scope of the topic and narrow down the focus

Questions I'm aiming to answer:

  • How is AI being used in the Film & TV industry?
  • What new technologies are threatening jobs in the industry?
  • How can AI be beneficial to creators and speed up processes?

To narrow down my scope of AI use in Film and TV, I started my search with Google Scholar. I didn't have much luck searching things like "Impact of AI in Film and TV," so using the Boolean operators under advanced search, I looked for resources with the following terms: AI, Film Industry dated after 2015 for more recent and accurate results. One paper I found was “Ghost in the (Hollywood) machine: Emergent applications of artificial intelligence in the film industry” by Pei-Sze Chow. From there, I looked at more recent articles which cited this paper and the sources cited within the paper itself. All of the resources I found useful, I stored in my Metadata and Justification Table excel spreadsheet. I found that I could narrow down my topic even further if I wanted to and look at AI used for commercial viability and green lighting productions, generative AI used in making films, new AI potentially threatening jobs, like those that create background actors or write scripts. I also decided I would focus on film, but include elements of media/TV if relevant for the user.
